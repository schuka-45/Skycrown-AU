Do You Have a Gambling Problem?

Gambling problems can affect anyone. Here are some warning signs that your gambling may have become a problem:

You spend more money or time gambling than you intended
You gamble to escape problems, stress or negative emotions
You lie to family or friends about how much you gamble
You have tried to cut down on gambling but couldn't
Gambling has caused financial difficulties or relationship problems
You feel anxious, irritable or restless when you're not gambling
You chase losses — gambling more to try to win back what you've lost
⚠️

If you answered yes to two or more of the above, we strongly encourage you to seek help. Gambling problems are treatable — reaching out is the first step. Use our self-exclusion tool or contact one of the free support services below.
